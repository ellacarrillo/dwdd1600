Why everyone has different taste in Music

Introversion vs. Extra-version

Introverts and extroverts often have different music taste. This is because of how their brains operate differently. Introverts perfer more calming and soothing music. Extroverts most of the time prefer exciting and more intense music. This has to do with dopamine and the different levels that are stored in their brains.

Most introverts use music for reflection and concentration. Some specific types of music that introverts like to listen to are, indie, folk, acoustic, and classical music. A lot of introverts like to listen to music alone and thorugh headphones.

Extroverts mostly gain music taste from social atmospheres. This can be from social media or parties. Some music that they like to listen to are, hip-hop, energetic songs, and songs that they can dance to. They also enjoy music more when it is playing in a social setting whether that would be at a party or concert.

Cultural Upbringing

  1. Family Influence plays a big part in cultural upbringing because of the certain types of music that your family and relatives listened to when you were a kid.
  2. Language is a big influence in cutural upbringing and people are more drawn to music that they can understand and connect with.
  3. Cultural traditions are tied to holidays, religion and any kind of celebration which in most cultures, all involve music in some way.
  4. Media exposure has a way of promoting music that is relevant to your country or a current event and very much invluences people to listen to a certain type of music.
