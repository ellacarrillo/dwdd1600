History behind it

Evolvement of Technology

Technology plays such a big role in how music has evolved over the years. Without technology, the influence of listening to music would never be a thing.

The Invention of the Radio
The radio became super popular in the early 1900s and completely transformed how people listen to music. Before the radio, people only heard music through live events, or physically played an instrument. Radio stations could broadcast music to a ton of people and this is where music took off in the world. It exposed so many different genres and other types of music that allowed poeple to get a taste of their own.
The Invention of the Discman
The Discman was invented in 1984 as a portable CD player. It closely followed the invention of CDs and it provided clearer sound to listen to rather than cassette tapes. The Discman allowed people to carry around their favorite albums and music which made listening to music a more personal experience to them.
The Invention of the MP3 Player
MP3 players were invented in the 1990s-2000s. People could carry thousands of songs in their pockets and did not have to carry around CDs. This caused music to become even more popular. Apple changed music listening in 2001 to create3 personal playlists and many music tastes became much more diverse because they were no longer limited to just CDs.
